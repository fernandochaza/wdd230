Overview

Site Name

Caleta Olivia Chamber of Commerce

Proposed Domain

www.caletachamberofcommerce.org

Purpose

The overall goal of the chamber of commerce site is to promote the business interests of the members of the organization and promote the area, attracting additional business, tourism, and people.

Audience

The site is intended for local businesses, business clients and tourists.

Scenarios

  • What upcoming events, workshops, and networking opportunities are happening in our city's business community?
  • What resources and programs are available?
  • What are the benefits of joining our chamber of commerce, and how can businesses apply?
  • What are the key attractions and activities that define the city's tourism offerings and points of interest?
  • What is the current weather condition, and what are the forecasts and seasonal climate expectations?
